## 3. General principles

We process the personal data of our website visitors only to the extent necessary to provide a functioning website and to deliver our content and services. Processing generally takes place only with the consent of the data subject, or where a legal basis exists under Art. 6 (1) GDPR.

## 4. Provision of the website and server log files

Each time the website is accessed, our hosting provider (see section 5) automatically collects data in server log files. The following data may be collected:

- Browser type and version
- The operating system of the accessing system
- The website from which an accessing system reaches our site (referrer)
- The pages accessed on our site
- Date and time of access
- The IP address (truncated/anonymised)
- The internet service provider of the accessing system

This data is used exclusively for the technical provision of the website, the security of our IT systems and anonymised statistical analysis. The data is not merged with other data sources.

**Legal basis:** Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in the secure provision of our website).
**Retention period:** Log files are automatically deleted after a maximum of 30 days.

## 9. Your rights as a data subject

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** (Art. 15 GDPR) — to the data stored about you
- **Rectification** (Art. 16 GDPR) — of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** (Art. 17 GDPR) — unless legal retention obligations apply
- **Restriction of processing** (Art. 18 GDPR)
- **Data portability** (Art. 20 GDPR) — to receive your data in a common format
- **Objection** (Art. 21 GDPR) — to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdrawal of consent** (Art. 7 (3) GDPR) — at any time, with effect for the future
- **Complaint** to the competent supervisory authority (see section 2)

To exercise your rights, an informal message to [info@fxconsulting.de](mailto:info@fxconsulting.de) is sufficient.

## 10. Automated decision-making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling within the meaning of Art. 22 GDPR.

## 11. Data security

We use transport-layer encryption (TLS/SSL) for website traffic, recognisable by the lock icon in your browser and the `https://` URL. This protects data transmitted between your device and our server against unauthorised access and tampering.
